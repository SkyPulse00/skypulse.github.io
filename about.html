CAMP KAINOMAYAN

Barangay San Juan, Botolan, Zambales

Camp Kainomayan, located in Botolan, Zambales, is a recreational facility that offers a variety of outdoor activities and serves as a gateway to several natural attractions in the area. The camp is situated near the Bucao River in Barangay San Juan and features a natural stream with year-round running cold and crystal-clear freshwater.

SUNSET VILLAS

Location: Barangay East Poblacion, Palauig, Zambales

Sunset Villas Palauig Zambales is a resort located in East Poblacion (Sitio Subic), Palauig, Zambales. The resort features an outdoor swimming pool, garden, terrace, and an on-site restaurant. Guests have access to a shared kitchen and can enjoy amenities such as free Wi-Fi and parking. The accommodations are designed in a Bali-inspired style, offering a unique beachfront experience.

ANAWANGIN COVE

Location: San Antonio, Zambales

Looking for complete fun and adventure, visit Anawangin Cove, situated in San Antonio, Zambales, is a crescent-shaped beach known for its distinctive scenery. Its shoreline is covered with grayish volcanic sand and lined with agoho trees, which resemble pine trees, giving the cove a unique charm. Surrounded by low hills, the cove features a stunning white sand beach.
