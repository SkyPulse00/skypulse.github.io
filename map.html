CAMP KAINOMAYAN

Barangay San Juan, Botolan, Zambales

Camp Kainomayan, located in Botolan, Zambales, is a recreational facility that offers a variety of outdoor activities and serves as a gateway to several natural attractions in the area. The camp is situated near the Bucao River in Barangay San Juan and features a natural stream with year-round running cold and crystal-clear freshwater.

SUNSET VILLAS

Location: Barangay East Poblacion, Palauig, Zambales

Sunset Villas Palauig Zambales is a resort located in East Poblacion (Sitio Subic), Palauig, Zambales. The resort features an outdoor swimming pool, garden, terrace, and an on-site restaurant. Guests have access to a shared kitchen and can enjoy amenities such as free Wi-Fi and parking. The accommodations are designed in a Bali-inspired style, offering a unique beachfront experience.

ANAWANGIN COVE

Location: Barangay Pundaquit, San Antonio, Zambales

Looking for complete fun and adventure, visit Anawangin Cove, situated in San Antonio, Zambales, is a crescent-shaped beach known for its distinctive scenery. Its shoreline is covered with grayish volcanic sand and lined with agoho trees, which resemble pine trees, giving the cove a unique charm. Surrounded by low hills, the cove features a stunning white sand beach.Visitors can enjoy camping by the beach, swimming in the clear waters, and exploring the nearby forest and marsh areas

BAGSIT RIVER

Location: Sitio Dampay-Barangay Salaza, Palauig, Zambales

Located in Sitio Dampay, Barangay Salaza, Palauig, Bagsit River is nestled within a lush forest, providing a tranquil setting ideal for relaxation and meditation. The river's clear waters and surrounding greenery make it a perfect spot for those seeking a peaceful retreat.

TUKAL-TUKAL FALLS

Location: Barangay Malomboy, Botolan, Zambales

Situated in Barangay Malomboy, Botolan, Tukal-Tukal Falls is a four-tiered waterfall system at the foot of Mt. Mangkirat. Reaching the falls involves an hour-long 4x4 vehicle ride from Camp Kainomayan, followed by a trek lasting between one to one and a half hours through grassy and rocky terrain.

SUNDOWNERS VILLAS

Location: Botolan, Zambales

Situated in Botolan, Zambales, Sundowners Beach Villas is a 2-hectare beachfront resort known for its whitewashed villas by the beach. The resort offers spacious villas ranging from one-bedroom beach villas to six-bedroom grand pool villas, accommodating couples, families, and groups.

POTIPOT ISLAND

Location: Candelaria, Zambales

Potipot Island is renowned for its clear waters and fine white sand. Visitors often enjoy wading in the water, walking along the beach, and capturing photos of the scenic surroundings.Potipot Island is less than 10 minutes away from Dawal Beach Resort, Candelaria Zambales. Boat ride is P400 (roundtrip, boats usually can accommodate a maximum of 6pax so 400/6 would be cheaper if you're travelling with friends). Entrance fee to the island is P100/pax.

CAPONES ISLAND

Location: Barangay Pundaquit, San Antonio, Zambales

Capones Island, also known as Isla de Capon Grande, is a small island located approximately 3.8 kilometers off the coast of San Antonio, Zambales.The island is renowned for its rugged cliffs, white sand beaches, and clear turquoise waters, making it a popular destination for beachgoers and snorkelers. Visitors can reach Capones Island via a short boat ride from the fishing village of Pundaquit, which typically takes about 10 minutes.

MAGALAWA ISLAND

Location: Barangay Salaza, Palauig, Zambales

Located in Palauig, Magalawa Island spans approximately 56 hectares and is accessible via a short boat ride from Sitio Luan. The island boasts creamy white sand beaches, towering coconut trees, and rich marine life, making it ideal for snorkeling and diving.For accommodations, Magalawa Island offers options such as the Armada Resort, which provides packages including meals, lodging, and activities.

AGBOBOTILYA RIVER

Location: Sitio Maporac-Barangay San Juan, Cabangan, Zambales

Agbobotilya River is renowned for its crystal-clear waters and serene environment. The river is a popular spot for swimming and relaxation, attracting both locals and tourists seeking a tranquil retreat. To reach the river, visitors typically undertake a hike lasting between one to two hours, traversing scenic landscapes.

BAKASYUNAN RESORT

Location: Barangay Bangantalinga, Iba, Zambales

Located in Iba, Zambales, Bakasyunan Resort spans a 3-hectare property with a 100-meter-long and 30-meter-wide beachfront. The resort offers a variety of accommodations, including beachfront villas with private pools, native-inspired nipa huts, and townhouses, catering to families, groups, and corporate events.

MT.TAPULAO

Location: Sitio-Dampay-Barangay, Salaza, Palauig, Zambales

Standing at 2,037 meters above sea level, Mt. Tapulao is the highest peak in the Zambales mountain range. Situated in Palauig, Zambales, the mountain is named after the "tapulao" or Sumatran pine trees that are abundant in the area. The trail to the summit spans approximately 18 kilometers, predominantly along a wide, rocky path that was once utilized for chromite mining operations.

MT.MARIGLEM

Location: Barangay New San Juan, Cabangan, Zambales

Mt. Mariglem, standing at 573 meters above sea level, is situated in Barangay New San Juan, Cabangan, Zambales. It's a relatively new hiking destination, having opened to the public in December 2023. The trail offers hikers a moderate challenge with a difficulty rating of 3/9, making it suitable for both beginners and seasoned hikers. Along the trek, adventurers are treated to lush trails, serene atmospheres, and panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.

MT.PINATUBO

Location: Barangay San Juan, Botolan, Zambales

Mt. Pinatubo has transformed into a popular tourist destination, renowned for its stunning crater lake with turquoise waters. The standard approach to the summit involves a combination of off-road vehicle travel and trekking, culminating in breathtaking views of the crater lake. The mountain's unique landscape, shaped by volcanic activity, offers a stark yet beautiful scenery that attracts numerous visitors annually.

MT.CALIB-UNGAN

Location: Barangay San Juan, Botolan, Zambales

Mt. Calib-ungan, located in Botolan, Zambales, is a favored spot among hiking enthusiasts. The mountain provides several trails that meander through its terrain, allowing hikers to immerse themselves in its natural beauty at their own pace. From its peak, one can enjoy panoramic vistas of lush green mountains and the shimmering blue waters of the West Philippine Sea. The area also offers opportunities for nature and bird-watching, making it a must-visit for nature lovers.

COTO MINES KIDZ POOL

Location: Coto Mines, Masinloc, Zambales

Situated in Masinloc, Coto Mines is known for its clear waters and natural attractions. The area offers camping opportunities, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in nature. It's an ideal spot for those seeking a serene environment away from the hustle and bustle.Visitors to Coto Mines can enjoy activities such as camping, nature walks, swimming and beach activities: Visitors can enjoy the clear waters for swimming and other water-related activities. Photography: With its beautiful landscapes and natural charm, it’s a great place for capturing photos. Coto Mines is often considered a hidden gem for those looking for a more secluded and peaceful experience away from the crowded tourist spots in Zambales.

FUNTASEA BEACH RESORT

Location: Barangay Sto.Rosario, Iba, Zambales

Located in Iba, Funtasea Beach Resort offers a blend of relaxation and recreation. The resort features various room types, including standard, family, and group accommodations.The resort includes amenities such as an infinity pool, a wellness spa, a restaurant with local and international dishes, and a chill bar with refreshing drinks.
